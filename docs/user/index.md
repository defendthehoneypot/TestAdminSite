## Overview

As an end user, you need to register your IP address before you can access your organization's VPN or protected resources from a new location. This is a quick, one-time process for each location.

## Quick Start

Need access right now? Here's the fast version:

<div class="step-card" markdown>

1. **Go to** [access.vespersecure.com](https://access.vespersecure.com){:target="_blank"}
2. **Log in** with your corporate credentials (Google, Microsoft, or Okta)
3. **Choose** Permanent (home/office) or Temporary (hotel/coffee shop)
4. **Wait** 5 minutes for approval and system update
5. **Connect** to VPN as normal

## When Do I Need This?

You need to register your IP address when:

- ‚úÖ **First time** accessing VPN from a new location
- ‚úÖ **Working from home** for the first time
- ‚úÖ **Traveling** and need to work remotely
- ‚úÖ **Changed locations** (moved, new office, etc.)
- ‚úÖ **Using a coffee shop, hotel, or public space**

You do **NOT** need to register:

- ‚ùå Every time you connect to VPN (only once per location)
- ‚ùå When using a permanent location you've already registered
- ‚ùå When already in the office on the corporate network

---

## What Information is Collected?

Vesper Secure only collects minimal information:

- Your corporate email/identity
- Your current IP address
- Location type (permanent or temporary)
- Registration timestamp

Your organization's administrator can see your registered locations.

---

## Need Help?

If you encounter issues:

1. Check the [User FAQ](faq.md) for common questions
2. Contact your **organization's IT support** (not Vesper Secure directly)
3. Provide them with:
   - Your email address
   - The IP address you're trying to register
   - Any error messages you received

!!! danger "Important"
    End users should contact their organization's IT support for help, not Vesper Secure directly.
